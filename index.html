ABOUT THE GIRLS

The Powerpuff Girls were created by Professor Utonium in an attempt to create the "perfect little girl" using a mixture of sugar, spice and everything nice. They fight crime and the forces of evil in the city of Townsville, USA, and always save the world before bedtime.
